## Summary

Paycheck First is designed to keep financial and work information on the user's device. The current version does not require an account, transmit user records to us, sell information, serve advertising, or use analytics or third-party crash reporting.

## Information handled by the app

Users may enter or generate information including:

- Income, wage, overtime, withholding, deduction, retirement, and savings settings
- Work shifts, clock-in and clock-out times, lunch time, tips, bonuses, and notes
- Bills, due dates, payment schedules, and payment history
- Debts, balances, interest rates, minimum payments, and payoff estimates
- Food, fuel, and other budgeting amounts
- App security and preference settings

This information is used only to provide the app's budgeting, work-tracking, calculation, reminder, and planning features.

## Local storage and encryption

User records are stored in the app's private storage on the device. Saved records are encrypted using AES-256-GCM with a device-bound key protected by Android Keystore.

Android application backup is disabled. The current version does not provide cloud synchronization or device-to-device transfer.

If Android cannot access the encryption key, the app stops automatic saving and does not intentionally overwrite the existing encrypted records.

## Biometrics and device authentication

Users may optionally require biometric or device authentication before editing or deleting bills and debts. Authentication is performed by Android. Paycheck First does not receive or store fingerprints, facial templates, device PINs, patterns, or passwords.

Deleting all local data always requires confirmation and device authentication.

## Widgets and notifications

The home-screen widget and active-shift notification may display work status, elapsed work time, lunch status, and punch controls. They do not display wages, tips, effective net income, bills, debts, or balances.

Android controls notification permission and lock-screen presentation. Users may change notification settings through Android.

## Location

The current version does not request or use device location. Automatic workplace geofencing is a possible future feature and is currently disabled. If location features are introduced, they will require explicit permission and an updated disclosure before use.

## Analytics, advertising, logs, and crash reports

The current version does not include advertising, analytics, remote logging, or third-party crash-reporting services. The app does not intentionally place financial values in diagnostic logs or exception messages.

Android or the device manufacturer may retain limited system diagnostics according to the user's device and account settings. We do not receive those diagnostics through the app.

## Sharing and sale of information

The current version does not transmit user records to us or third parties. We do not sell or share user financial or work information.

Future encrypted export or device-transfer functionality is currently disabled. Any future release of that functionality will require an updated disclosure and explicit user action.

## Retention and deletion

Information remains on the device until the user edits or deletes it, selects **Settings > Delete all local data**, clears the app's storage, or uninstalls the app.

The in-app deletion control permanently removes saved records and the local encryption key. Deleted information cannot be recovered by the app.

Because the current version does not transmit user records to us, we do not maintain a server-side copy to retrieve or delete.

## Security limitations

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate for local financial information, but no device or software can be guaranteed completely secure. Device compromise, operating-system vulnerabilities, or access to an unlocked phone may reduce these protections.

## Children's privacy

Paycheck First is a general-audience budgeting tool and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect children's personal information through a server or account system.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, legal requirements, or data practices change. The effective date will be updated, and material changes will be disclosed through the app or its store listing when appropriate.
